Data dictionary:

startTime: Time I started to make the coffee
date: Date I made the coffee
count: Number of beans I intended to count out
over: Number of beans over (if +) or under (if -) I counted out
cTime: Amount of time it took me to count out *count* amount of beans
gTime: Amount of time it took me to manually grind the beans
bTime: Amount of time it took me to brew (pour water over) the coffee
coffee: Name of coffee I used
endTime: Time I stopped brewing (once all water had passed through the beans)
notes: Random notes I took for each record
